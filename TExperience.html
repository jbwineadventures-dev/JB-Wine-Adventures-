A Curated Journey

Tuscany as few travellers experience it

For those who live life passionately — this intimate eight-day journey weaves together Renaissance Florence, the legendary wine estates of Chianti and Montalcino, and the timeless beauty of Siena's hill towns into one unforgettable experience.

With just ten guests, every moment is thoughtfully curated. Private access, unhurried tastings, Michelin-recognised dining, and two WSET-certified hosts who have explored the world in pursuit of extraordinary food and wine.

Journey Highlights

Seven reasons to say sì

01
Private Uffizi & Duomo

Priority entry and personal headsets through the world's greatest Renaissance collection — no queues, no crowds, pure immersion.

02
Brunello Vertical Tasting

A private vertical of four Brunello di Montalcino vintages at Capanna — one of the founding estates of this legendary DOCG.

03
Truffle Hunting in Chianti

Walk forested Chianti hills with an expert hunter and trained dog — uncovering Tuscany's most prized culinary treasure.

04
Michelin Dining Experiences

Three elevated dining experiences — including a seven-course tasting menu at Gallo Nero, Michelin-starred, in the heart of Siena.

05
Pasta Making Masterclass

Afternoon at Accademia restaurant with expert chefs learning how to make traditional pasta — then dine on your creations paired with estate wines.

06
San Gimignano & Vernaccia

Explore this UNESCO hilltop town then taste the celebrated Vernaccia di San Gimignano at a nearby estate over a traditional Tuscan lunch.

07
Luxury Throughout

Four and five-star stays across Florence, Siena, and Hotel Le Fontanelle — with private Mercedes transfers and daily breakfast included.

The Wines of Tuscany

From Chianti
to Brunello

Tuscany is home to some of the world's most celebrated wines. Your WSET-certified hosts guide you through each terroir, deepening appreciation at every pour — from first sip to final glass.

  • Chianti Classico — the soul of Sangiovese
  • Brunello di Montalcino — Italy's most powerful red
  • Vernaccia di San Gimignano — Tuscany's distinguished white
  • Super Tuscans — bold, boundary-breaking blends
  • Boutique estate discoveries in the Chianti hills
  • Curated wine library tasting in historic Florence

Day by Day

Your Eight-Day Journey

Florence · Chianti · Siena · Montalcino · San Gimignano · September 18–26

Day 1 · Sept 18
Arrival in Florence

Private luxury transfer to Kraft Hotel. Welcome reception on the rooftop bar with Tuscan wines and hors d'oeuvres overlooking the city.

Day 2 · Sept 19
Discover Florence & The Duomo

Private guided walking tour — the magnificent Duomo complex, Giotto's Bell Tower, Piazza della Signoria. Michelin-recognised dinner with wine pairing.

Day 3 · Sept 20
Renaissance Masterpieces

Private Uffizi Gallery tour with priority entry and personal headsets. Curated wine library tasting. Michelin-starred dinner celebrating exceptional Tuscan flavours.

Day 4 · Sept 21
Tuscan Cooking Experience

Free morning along the Arno River. Hands-on pasta-making at Accademia with expert chefs. Wine-paired dinner — your own fresh creations on the plate.

Day 5 · Sept 22
Truffle Hunting & Chianti Wines

Truffle hunt in the Chianti hills. Lunch and tasting at Gagliole Winery. Guided tour of Siena Cathedral. Dinner at Michelin-recommended Osteria Le Logge.

Day 6 · Sept 23
Brunello di Montalcino

Private vertical tasting of four Brunello vintages at founding estate Capanna Winery. Free time in charming Montalcino. Seven-course menu at Michelin-starred Gallo Nero.

Day 7 · Sept 24
San Gimignano & Chianti

UNESCO hilltop town exploration followed by Vernaccia di San Gimignano estate wine tasting and lunch. Afternoon arrival at the magnificent five-star Hotel Le Fontanelle in the Chianti countryside.

Day 8 · Sept 25
A Day in the Countryside

Relax at Le Fontanelle — pool, spa, vineyard walks, cycling. Farewell dinner at Ristorante La Colonna with curated wine pairings.

Day 9 · Sept 26
Departure — Arrivederci Toscana

After breakfast, private luxury Mercedes transfer to Florence Airport. Until the next adventure.
